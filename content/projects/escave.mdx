**Escave** is a mobile IOS AR scavenger-hunt and riddle game. Solve daily clues, use object detection to discover in-world artifacts, and collect 3D scans you can show off as trophies.

### Escave+ perks

- ad-free Hints
- 1 of each power-up monthly
- 2 escave tokens used for downloading the 3D assets
